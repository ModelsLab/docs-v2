# Privacy Policy

## Overview

At ModelsLab, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Username
- Profile information you provide

### Usage Data
- API usage statistics
- Generated content metadata
- Service interaction logs

### Technical Information
- IP addresses
- Browser information
- Device identifiers

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve our services
- Process API requests
- Communicate with you about your account
- Ensure platform security
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring
- Secure data centers

## Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal requirements.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
